Description

Maharshi is an upcoming 2019 Indian Telugu film written and directed by Vamsi Paidipally and produced by C. Ashwini Dutt, Dil Raju and Prasad V. Potluri, under the banners of Vyjayanthi Movies, Sri Venkateswara Creations and PVP Cinema. The film stars Mahesh Babu, Pooja Hegde and Sonal Chauhan in lead roles, with Allari Naresh and Meenakshi Dixit acting in a prominent role. The music of the film is composed by Devi Sri Prasad.[2] It is to be released on 25 April 2019.
